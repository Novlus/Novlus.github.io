Interoperability Project for the IUT Nancy Charlemagne

Let's talk about the two projects made in interoperability. The first project is used to know the weather of the day in Nancy, the different stations of stan bikes and the address of my house on a map as well as the air quality of Nancy. All this is possible thanks to different APIs that give this information in real time.

The second project also allows to know the air quality of Nancy. But they also give us other information as the information of the Covid in the big East but also the traffic problems in the big Nancy. Again this information is obtained through APIs.
