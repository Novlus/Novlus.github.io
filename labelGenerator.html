Label Generator Project for the CHRU of Nancy

last year during my internship for the CHRU of Nancy on the Administrative, Economic, logistical and Financial management of the patient team (GAEL) I had to create a generator of pre-numbered labels. These labels are used to monitor the patient throughout his stay at the CHRU. To create this label generator it was necessary to create a form in PHP with an error check in javascript. Once the form is completed, a pdf is generated with the sheets of labels to print.

I will still explain to you how the program works.
The first field of the form is used to choose the number of patients for which labels must be generated. The maximum limit being 100. The comment part is used to explain why these labels were created. You can choose whether you want one label sheet or two per patient. but you will have a limit of 100 maximum boards. Finally, you must choose the establishment for which you are generating these sheets of labels.
the generated labels part is used to see all the generated labels. Know the reason for the generation of the labels. When they were generated and whether they were used or not
